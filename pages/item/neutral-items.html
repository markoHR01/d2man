Using a stored rune fully refills the Bottle.

Tier:1
Ability:Passive

Neutral Artifacts you craft while holding a Dormant Curio have their potency increased by 30%.

  • Certain stats on the next artifact will be amplified. Will not amplify cooldown, mana cost, or cast range.

Mana Draught

Tier:2
Ability:No Target
Affects:Allied Heroes

Active: Life Bottoms Up

Restores 70 + 3% of the caster's maximum mana over 6 seconds. If the hero is attacked by an enemy hero or Roshan, the effect is lost.

Hold Control to use on an allied hero.

Cooldown:60
Range:650

Passive: Fill Up

Cooldown Speed of Mana Draught is increased by 30% while in water.

Poor Man's Shield

Tier:2
Ability:Passive

Passive: Damage Block

Gives a 100% chance to block 30 damage from incoming attacks on melee heroes, and 20 damage on ranged.

Has 50% chance to block damage from creeps.

  • Multiple sources of damage block do not stack.

Searing Signet

Tier:2
Ability:Passive

Passive: Burn Through

Instances of magic damage from spells over 55 ignites enemies, causing them to take 90 magic damage over 6 seconds. Burn damage against non-hero targets is decreased by 50%.

  • Will not ignite enemies that are more than 2000 range away.

Tumbler's Toy

Tier:2
Ability:No Target

Active: Vault

Propels your hero forward 300 units. Tumbler's Toy gets disabled for 3 seconds if its owner receives damage from a player source.

  • Can't be cast while rooted.

Defiant Shell

Tier:2
Ability:Passive

Passive: Reciprocity

When attacked, the hero counter-attacks a target within their attack range for 80% of their regular attack damage.

Cooldown:5
