Instantaneously swaps positions with a target Hero, friend or enemy. Nether Swap interrupts channeling abilities on the target. Enemy Swapped units take damage. Vengeful Spirit and allied swapped units gain a barrier equal to the damage dealt.

Retribution

Ability Upgrade - Vengeance Aura

Upon death, creates a strong illusion of Vengeful Spirit that deals full damage, takes increased damage, and can cast all of her spells. If the illusion is alive when Vengeful Spirit respawns, she will take its place. XP earned by her illusion is given to her. Strong Illusions are not instantly killed by spells.

Illusion Damage Taken:115%
